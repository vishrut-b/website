Welcome!

I am Vishrut Bezbarua, a PhD candidate in Astrophysics at Laboratoire d'astrophysique de Marseille under Dr. Nicolas Laporte and Dr. Olivier Ilbert, studying the properties of the first protoclusters and their role in the Epoch of Reionization, using data from JWST and Euclid. Previously, I have worked with Dr. Pierre Ocvirk and Dr. Katarina Kraljic for my master's thesis, where I attmpted to quantify the impact of inhomogeneous reionization on cosmic filaments and haloes, using Cosmic Dawn III simulation data.
